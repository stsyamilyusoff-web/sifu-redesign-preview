Account · Application status

The account/application review screen, reached from the profile status banner. Not a support ticket, so it keeps account vocabulary (no ticket/request rename). Canonical: a StatusBanner-family state summary (the colour carrier, one per state), a vertical timeline stepper (Application sent · In review · Account ready, fixed at In review), and per-state notice cards + CTAs. Four live states preserved: incomplete (Continue your application), un-verified (in review, shown), suspended (shown), terminated (appeal). Colour rationed: neutral for in-review, amber for the suspended caution; the stepper uses brand tint for structure. Dates one format DD-MMM-YYYY. No field dropped. True 402×874, light + dark.
