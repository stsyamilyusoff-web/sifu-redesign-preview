Support · End my engagement (the 4-step resignation)

Reached only after the Discouragement deterrent. A guided 4-step flow, Why · When · Notes · Survey, so a leaving tutor is heard and admin gets a clean record. Canonical: centered nav, a bar progress stepper (no word labels, so it never repeats the step title), single-select chips, a sticky Next/Submit, and a confirm alert ("you can't undo this"). Three real guard states (suspended / not eligible / already in review) render instead of the form when the backend blocks it. Steps 3 and 4 are skippable. Dates one format DD-MMM-YYYY. No em-dashes, no duplication. True 402×874, light + dark.
Tint = progress + selected chip + primary action Amber = the guard-state caution Neutral = unselected chips, skip, helpers
