Proposal notes

1
Distance beats a city list. A pin + radius matches by actual travel distance, so a PJ tutor willing to travel 10 km also catches nearby Subang / Shah Alam requests without hand-picking every city. More precise for both sides.
2
Gated on mode, cleanly. Online = no area (fixes the live "cities required for Online" oddness). In-person / Hybrid = the area. Multiple areas allowed for tutors who teach around two bases.
3
Coverage read-out. The picker names the towns the radius covers ("about PJ, Subang and Shah Alam") so the tutor understands their reach before confirming, not just an abstract circle.
