Notes for Hafiz
Left alone on purpose: the active-class flow (clock-in with photo proof, the timer, clock-out, crash-recovery) is already solid. We do not touch what works.
Backend dependencies: (1) the per-class EARNING in the class-list payload (shared by the schedule + earnings, build once); (2) the payout DATE + method on the earnings endpoint; (3) a lighter report schema (one read + open text) replacing the six fixed fields; (4) cross-session PROGRESS per student.
Open questions: is the rate always flat RM18, or does it vary by level/subject; the payout cadence (monthly on the 1st?); whether the lighter report keeps any of the old structured fields for internal analytics.
Scope: the tutor's delivery loop, teach, report, get paid. The schedule cockpit and earnings are the retention levers; reports lose their friction; the solid clock-in/out stays. This closes the tutor-side funnel after Activate, the profile, and winning a job.
