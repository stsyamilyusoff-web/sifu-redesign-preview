Support · Discouragement (the leave / delete deterrent)

A friction screen shown once before a resignation or an account deletion, so a tutor gets a moment to reconsider. Lifecycle-aware: a brand-new tutor sees a gentle milestone, a veteran sees the volume they have built (tenure, classes, students, review, the same objective numbers the profile shows), and account-deletion shows exactly what is lost. The action stack nudges to "Chat with us first" (the retention deflection is the emphasised primary; the destructive path is de-emphasised). Data-viz stays illustration/numbers (per the photography rule); no invented stats, an unverified tutor with no data sees a supportive empty variant instead. No em-dashes, no duplication.
