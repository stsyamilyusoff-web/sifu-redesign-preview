Deliver · Camera proof + Home demand card (2026-07-01)

Screen 1 — CameraScreen: the stock proof-capture surface reached during clock-in and clock-out. The live app opens the native camera with no UI context, so the tutor has no reminder of what they are capturing or why. This mock adds a minimal overlay: a close control, a one-line hint pill, the capture button, and a flip-camera control. Everything else stays out of the way. Viewfinder fills the full frame (no inset padding).

Screen 2 — Home / DemandSummaryCard: the card was previously a passive stat widget that went nowhere. Founder decision (2026-07-01): the card is now a tappable funnel entry into the Job Ticket (request) feed. The whole card is one tap target with a trailing chevron and a "View open requests" affordance at the bottom. It surfaces real matching demand (subject breakdown, area) and reads as actionable, not hype. Tint accent on the card is intentional (content card, not a button). Green is reserved for status only. Three frames: light Home, dark Home, and a close-up of the demand card in dark.

Token note: --camera-bg declared in :root for the viewfinder near-black. No raw hex in markup. All other values use the shared token set from deliver-active-class.html. Sample data throughout.
