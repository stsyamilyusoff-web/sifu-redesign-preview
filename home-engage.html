Why two states. A dashboard that leads with RM0 and a 0 rating discourages the exact tutor we most need to activate. The new-tutor Home swaps recognition-of-the-past for assurance + a clear first step, then hands the established treatment over the moment they have a class and earnings.
Notes for Hafiz
The angle. Home is the daily driver of applications (the North Star). It opens with the four things a worker needs: assurance (recognition), motivation (a goal), drive (the matches), confidence (a warm, personal greeting). Grounded in how Grab and Uber driver Homes work.
Earned, not projected. "RM480 earned this month" is real commission from verified sessions (already computed as cumulative_commission), shown as the reward for work done. We do NOT show speculative "up to RM X if you win" pressure.
Tenure-adaptive. Established leads with standing + earned earnings + the weekly goal. New leads with "Verified, ready" + a first-class nudge, never an empty RM0 grid.
Backend deps. The standing fields (rating, classes-taught, parents-chosen), the weekly-goal count, and pending_reports_count + pending_approvals_count for "what you owe". The matches count + subjects already come from demand-summary; earnings + verified hours already come from home-data.
Honest + neutral. No fake streaks, no manufactured urgency, no income-targeting pressure. Adopt the goal-and-recognition mechanic from the best gig apps, not their manipulation.
