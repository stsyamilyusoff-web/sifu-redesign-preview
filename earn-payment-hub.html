The "where is my money" surface, the #3 funnel leak (earnings transparency, payout clarity, an on-track signal). The live hub is two stacked cards with no lifetime context and no sense of when the next payout lands. The redesign leads with a clear earnings summary (paid this year, with a paid vs pending split), shows the pending amount once (in the hero split), with a lean tappable row into the class-by-class detail, and keeps the monthly history. No repeated status labels, no duplicate amount, no promised date; the payout cadence lives on the detail (in-app). Money strips a trailing .00 so amounts stay clean as they grow to 4 to 6 digits, keeping real cents when present (RM 94.50); the shareable PaymentLedger receipt keeps full 2dp for reconciliation. Tokens only, no em-dashes, light + dark. Colour budget for money status: green = paid / earned, amber = pending (the one waiting flag), brand = the hero and actions, neutral otherwise.
