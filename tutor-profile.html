Tutor profile · the three faces
The profile a parent uses to CHOOSE, the profile a tutor MANAGES, and the tool an admin uses to VERIFY. North Star: a profile good enough that parents pick in-app, replacing the manual WhatsApp hand-pick, which lifts wins and applications. For Hafiz · 24 June 2026.
Every "choose this person" profile (Preply, Airbnb, Fiverr, Care.com) runs the same skeleton, and the ones that work without many reviews lean on three things: a verified badge, a single computed tier, and objective volume numbers. That is exactly our asset mix, so the tutor card leans on video → verified → tier → track record, and designs the empty star block out (an empty 0.0 destroys trust). Sifututor's model is curated parent-choice: the admin verifies, the platform shortlists, the parent picks. This profile is what makes that pick confident.

Why this order. The framework, adapted to our assets:

1 Video hero · the number-one trust object, it replaces stars. Most parents decide in the first 15 seconds (Preply).

2 Verified + tier badges · the strength tier is the stars-replacement, one glance, high trust (Fiverr level, Airbnb Superhost).

3 Four stat chips · the objective moat: classes, hours, approval, response. No stars needed (Wyzant hours, Fiverr response rate).

4 CTA · pinned, choose without scrolling.

Below the fold · itemised verification (Care.com's whole model), subjects and levels, a short specific about.

No empty stars · an honest "reviews after the first class" line, never a 0.0 block (which actively destroys trust).
